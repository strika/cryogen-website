Hi there!

Thanks for stopping by. My name is Nebojša Stričević, but friends call me Strika. So should you.

I live in Novi Sad, Serbia, with my wonderful wife Tanja and son Mihajlo.

I was lucky enough to own Commodore 64 when I was a kid. And even more that it came with a book called "What Can Commodore 64 Do?", that I read so many times. Since then my love for the world of computers never ended.

These days I'm working as a web engineer at Rendered Text, Novi Sad.

In free time, I enjoy coffee and black tea, dark chocolate, dark beer and pelinkovac. I'm also interested in mechanical watches and older cars.

My long term life goal is to increase the number of stars I can see each day.
